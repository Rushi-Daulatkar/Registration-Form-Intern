INTERNSHIP AGREEMENT POLICY WELCOME TO THE MOTION CUT INTERNSHIP PROGRAM!
We are excited to offer you the opportunity to gain hands-on experience in your chosen field through our task-based internship program. Please read the following agreement carefully before applying.
ABOUT MOTION CUT:
At Motion Cut, we specialize in providing digital products such as VFX, Royalty-free music, sound effects, and LUTs. We also offer internship opportunities in various fields, including Graphic Designing, Web Development, Frontend Web Development, Python Programming, Java Programming, and UI/UX Designing. Our commitment is to empower aspiring professionals like you and help you build your career.
INTERNSHIP PROGRAM DETAILS:
Nature of the Internship: The Motion Cut internship is task-based. Interns will be assigned real tasks relevant to their chosen field.
Duration: The duration of the internship varies based on the project assigned and typically ranges from weeks to months.
Registration Fee: To participate in our internship program, a one-time, non-refundable registration fee of 200 INR is required.

EXPECTATIONS AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
Task Completion: Interns are expected to complete tasks assigned to them in a timely and professional manner.
Professionalism: Maintain a professional attitude, meet deadlines, and communicate effectively with your assigned supervisor.
Training: Please note that Motion Cut does not provide formal training as part of this internship program. We believe in learning by doing.

BENEFITS OF THE MOTION CUT INTERNSHIP:
Hands-On Experience: Gain practical experience by working on real projects that will enhance your skills.
Portfolio Development: Build an impressive portfolio of work to showcase your accomplishments.
Affiliate Program: As an intern, you have the opportunity to participate in our affiliate program, where you can earn a generous 35% commission on sales generated through your referrals.

APPLICATION PROCESS:
Pay the 200 INR registration fee.
Complete the internship application form available on our website.
Once accepted, you will be assigned tasks based on your field of interest.

NO REFUND:
Please note that the registration fee is non-refundable. Once you've paid it, there will be no refunds.

IMPORTANT NOTES:
This internship is not a formal employment contract and does not guarantee future employment with Motion Cut.
Participants are required to follow all company policies and guidelines during their internship.
Motion Cut reserves the right to terminate the internship at any time for violations of company policies.
